Domino's Pizza, Inc.[6] is an American multinational pizza restaurant chain founded in 1960 and led by CEO Richard Allison. The corporation is Delaware domiciled[7] and headquartered at the Domino's Farms Office Park in Ann Arbor, Michigan.[3][5] As of 2018, Domino's had approximately 15,000 stores, with 5,649 in the U.S., 1,232 in India, and 1,094 in the U.K.[8][9] Domino's has stores in over 83 countries[10] and 5,701 cities worldwide.[11]
